FAQs



1. What are the various mode of payment accepted here ?
You can make payment Via Cash, Master Card, Visa Card, Debit Cards, Cheques, American Express Card, Credit Card.

2. Which is the nearest landmark ?
You can easily locate the establishment as it is in close proximity to Aarna Diagnostics.

3. Do I need to make reservation ?
Reservations are not required but they are strongly recommended especially in a high season (1st of December till 1st of February).

4. Is parking facility available ?
A separate parking space has been alloted for 2-wheelers and 4-wheelers.

5. Is it a good choice for vegetarians as well ?
The restaurant has separate kitchens and halls for both vegetarian and non-vegetarian customers.
